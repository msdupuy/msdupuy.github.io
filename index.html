About me

I am a maître de conférences (associate professor) at the Laboratoire Jacques-Louis LIONS (16-26-304) in Sorbonne Université (Paris, France). I was previously a postdoctoral researcher in TU Munich (Germany) with Gero Friesecke on the grant SFB/TRR 109 Discretization in Geometry and Dynamics. I did my PhD in Université Paris-Diderot under the supervision of Xavier Blanc.

My research focuses on the analysis of models and numerical methods for molecular systems or materials.

I also have worked on tensor trains for high-dimensional problems. Lecture notes on this topic are available here.
